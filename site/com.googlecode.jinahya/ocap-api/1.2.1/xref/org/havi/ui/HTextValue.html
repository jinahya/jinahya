10     This interface is implemented by all HAVi UI components which have
11     have editable text content (e.g. a text entry control).
12  
13     <h3>Event Behavior</h3>
14  
15     Subclasses of {@link org.havi.ui.HComponent HComponent} which
16     implement {@link org.havi.ui.HTextValue HTextValue} must respond to
17     {@link org.havi.ui.event.HFocusEvent HFocusEvent}, {@link 
18     org.havi.ui.event.HKeyEvent HKeyEvent}
19      and {@link
20     org.havi.ui.event.HTextEvent HTextEvent} events.
21  
22     <p> 
23     Applications should assume that classes which implement {@link
24     org.havi.ui.HTextValue HTextValue} can generate events of the
25     types {@link org.havi.ui.event.HFocusEvent HFocusEvent} and {@link
26     org.havi.ui.event.HTextEvent HTextEvent} in response to other
27     types of input event.
28  
29     <p> 
30     An application may add one or more {@link
31     org.havi.ui.event.HTextListener HTextListener} listeners to the
32     component. The {@link org.havi.ui.event.HTextListener#textChanged
33     textChanged} method of the {@link org.havi.ui.event.HTextListener
34     HTextListener} is invoked whenever the text in the {@link
35     org.havi.ui.HTextValue HTextValue} is changed, and the {@link
36     org.havi.ui.event.HTextListener#caretMoved caretMoved} method of the
37     {@link org.havi.ui.event.HTextListener HTextListener} is invoked whenever
38     the text caret position is altered.
39  
40     <p> 
41     HAVi text events are discussed in detail in the {@link
42     org.havi.ui.HKeyboardInputPreferred HKeyboardInputPreferred}
43     interface description.
44
