53       * A value for use in the preference field of the {@link
54       * org.havi.ui.HScreenConfigTemplate#setPreference setPreference}
55       * and {@link
56       * org.havi.ui.HScreenConfigTemplate#getPreferencePriority
57       * getPreferencePriority} methods in the {@link
58       * org.havi.ui.HGraphicsConfigTemplate HGraphicsConfigTemplate}
59       * that indicates that the graphics configuration should or shall
60       * support transparency in the graphics system such that the
61       * output of a video decoder is visible. This includes the
62       * following configurations :-
63       * <ul>
64       * <li>Configurations where there is a well defined transformation
65       * between video pixels and graphics pixels (e.g. pixels are the
66       * same size).
67       * <li>Configurations where an application displays graphics over
68       * video but where the video is considered as a background and
69       * hence no transformation between the two sets of pixels is
70       * required.
71       * </ul>     
72       * <p>
73       * Applications may specify a particular video configuration with
74       * which mixing must be supported. In this case, the video
75       * configuration is specified as an {@link
76       * org.havi.ui.HVideoConfiguration HVideoConfiguration} object. If
77       * no specific video configuration is required then it is not
78       * required to specify such a configuration and null can be used.
79       * <p>
80       * This preference is used by the platform as a constraint in
81       * selecting configurations. Templates generated by the platform
82       * and then returned to applications (e.g. from a
83       * <code>getConfigTemplate</code> method) shall not have this
84       * preference filled in by the platform.    
85       */
