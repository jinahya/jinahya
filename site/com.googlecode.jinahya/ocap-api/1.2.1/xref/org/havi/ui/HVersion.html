8   /**

11   * java.lang.System method getProperty, with the appropriate property
12   * name.

16   * HAVI_IMPLEMENTATION_VERSION strings.
17   * <p>
18   * In MHP, a call to <code>getProperty()</code> when referencing the

21   * <table border>
22   *   <tr><th>Constant</th><th>Value</th></tr>
23   *   <tr><td>HAVI_SPECIFICATION_VENDOR</td>

25   *   <tr><td>HAVI_SPECIFICATION_NAME</td>

28   *       <td>&quot;&lt;version&gt;&quot;</td></tr>
29   * </table>

31   * HAVi implementation is conformant, e.g.: &quot;1.0&quot; or
